# Ridge Mystery Drop 📦

A tongue-in-cheek idle gacha game. Open mystery packages, collect Ridge products, and try to complete your Collector's Booklet before the next standup.

---

## What is this?

Ridge Mystery Drop is a browser-based idle game where you:

- **Place orders** for mystery packages across four tiers (Standard → Express → Priority → Overnight)
- **Wait for real-time countdowns** as your shipments arrive
- **Open packages** to discover Ridge products across 4 rarities — 18 items total
- **Earn credits** from each unboxing to fund your next order
- **Upgrade carriers** to slash shipping times (USPS → UPS → FedEx → DHL at 5× speed)
- **Complete the Collector's Booklet** — 18 items across Common / Uncommon / Rare / Ultra Rare
- **Prestige** after collecting everything, resetting your stash for a permanent +10% rare rate bonus

Credits trickle in passively (1 per 10 seconds), so it rewards leaving the tab open during your meeting.

---

## Package Tiers

| Tier | Cost | Delivery | Rare Odds |
|------|------|----------|-----------|
| Standard | 20 cr | ~40 sec | Common-weighted (82% common) |
| Express | 30 cr | ~30 sec | Balanced (60% common, +10% rare boost) |
| Priority | 50 cr | ~15 sec | Balanced (+25% rare boost) |
| Overnight | 100 cr | ~5 sec | No commons — 55% uncommon, 35% rare, 10% ultra |

All tiers award base credits on open (10 / 15 / 25 / 50 cr respectively), plus the item's credit value. Duplicate items refund bonus credits instead.

---

## Rarities & Items

**18 collectibles total:**

- **Common (5)** — wallets and keycases, 12 cr value
- **Uncommon (5)** — collab wallets, power bank, ring, tracker cards, 28 cr value
- **Rare (4)** — limited wallets and bags, 65 cr value
- **Ultra Rare (4)** — iridescent wallet, MKBHD Vapor wallet, Mr. Doodle carry-on, 180–300 cr value

The **MKBHD Vapor** wallet doubles all credits earned from a package. The **Mr. Doodle carry-on** is pity-guaranteed on your 101st pull if you haven't received it yet.

## Carrier Upgrades

Purchased in the order panel — upgrades are permanent and carry over after Prestige.

| Carrier | Speed |
|---------|-------|
| USPS | 1× (default) |
| UPS | ~2× faster — 75 cr |
| FedEx | ~3× faster — 190 cr |
| DHL | 5× faster — 650 cr |

---

## Missions & Trophies

Complete missions to earn bonus credits and trophies. Trophy tiers from lowest to highest:

**Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Diamond → Iridescent**

Plus one special black-and-white **Doodle God** trophy awarded for pulling the Mr. Doodle carry-on.

---

## Other Features

- **Warehouse Store** — spend credits on wall colours, cat colours, Sean's shirt, props, and warehouse workers
- **Warehouse lighting** changes automatically based on your real local time (dawn / day / dusk / night)
- **Sound toggle** via the 🔊 button in the top bar
- **Unique save URL** — each session gets a `?s=` parameter; share or bookmark it to pick up where you left off
